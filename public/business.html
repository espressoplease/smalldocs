SmallDocs for business

Bring SmallDocs to your team

SmallDocs is free for personal use. For teams and companies we are building a set of capabilities around control, security, and brand, so the documents your agents produce stay private, on-brand, and easy to govern.

Vault

A private, shared home for your team's documents. Central storage and distribution of company-standard files, instead of links scattered across chats.

Private domains

Serve and share documents from your own domain, for example docs.yourcompany.com, rather than smalldocs.org.

Dedicated encryption keys

Your own keys for shared and stored documents, isolated from every other account, so the encryption boundary is yours alone.

Brand styling

House styles, your fonts, colours, and spacing, applied across every document your team creates, so output is on-brand without manual work.

Documents API

Generate, render, and export SmallDocs programmatically from your own systems and pipelines, the same documents your people read in the browser.

SSO & access control

Sign in with your identity provider and control who can read, edit, and share. Audit and retention controls for the documents that matter.
