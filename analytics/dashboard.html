Each row is a cohort — visitors who first came the same week. Cells are the number of page-load visits that cohort generated each week. We don't track unique users; one person revisiting many times counts as many visits. Weeks begin Monday (UTC, ISO 8601), applied to both the cohort table and the charts below. Counts are the sum of two deployments: sdocs.dev (this project's home until June 2026) and smalldocs.org. How this works
